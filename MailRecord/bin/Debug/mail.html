Subject: China - China | Changes to Approach, Arrival and Departure Procedures Published by AIP Amendment 4a/15 - effective 2 April 2015. (NavData Alerts)

 

Jeppesen NavData for cycle 1504, effective 02 April 2015, contains outdated Approach, Arrival and Departure Procedures. PR of China published AIP Amendment 4a/15, which introduces and changes a number of procedures. The Amendment was received after the date specified by ICAO for aeronautical information distribution. As a result, all Arrival and Departure Procedures for Chongqing, PR of China (ZUCK), and all Arrival, Departure and Approach Procedures for Guiyang, PR of China (ZUGY) are unusable. The procedure coding for the above mentioned procedures could not be processed for cycle 1504. THEREFORE, DO NOT USE THE ARRIVAL AND DEPARTURE PROCEDURES FOR (ZUCK), AND THE APPROACH, ARRIVAL AND DEPARTURE PROCEDURES FOR (ZUGY) IN CYCLE 1504 NAVDATA. Revised coding will appear in Jeppesen NavData for cycle 1505, effective 30 Apr
